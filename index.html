The ESTEEM Program

The ESTEEM program is a unique, one-year master's degree that empowers graduates of science, engineering, and technology disciplines to become entrepreneurs and innovators. It combines a rigorous curriculum with hands-on projects, giving students the tools to launch new ventures and drive innovation within existing companies.

Key areas of study include:

  • Venture Strategy & Business Planning
  • Financial Modeling & Valuation
  • Intellectual Property & Legal Foundations
  • Technology Commercialization
  • Design Thinking & Innovation

My capstone project is focused on developing a business model for a new technology aimed at improving patient outcomes in the healthcare sector.
